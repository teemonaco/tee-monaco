User Story

Mobile App for Data Sharing and Content Management

As a researcher working for a top pharmaceutical company, I need to stay updated with the latest research and data insights relevant to my field so that I can make informed decisions and stay ahead in my research.

Discover
The Discover screen serves as the central hub for researchers, showcasing curated topics like CI Newsletters and UCB Drugs. Users can favorite topics to personalize their feed, ensuring they stay updated on the latest research and insights relevant to their field.
Share and Save Articles
The Discover screen features enhanced functionality with Share and Save options for articles. Users can quickly share important articles with colleagues or bookmark them for future reference, making it easy to manage and distribute key research findings.
Filter Relevant Insights
This screen allows researchers to refine their search results using filters such as Content Category, Organization, Country, and more. This functionality helps users quickly narrow down vast amounts of information to find the most relevant articles and data for their specific research needs.
Semantic & Boolean Search
The Search screen provides a detailed search functionality, allowing users to enter specific keywords and view relevant articles. Advanced search options and relevance toggles enable users to effectively find and prioritize critical research information.
Personalized Feed
The My Stream screen provides a personalized feed of articles based on the user's selected topics and preferences. This ensures that researchers have easy access to the latest updates and insights tailored to their specific research interests, enhancing their ability to make informed decisions.
Customizable Settings
This screen allows users to customize their notification preferences, including setting urgency thresholds and selecting favorite topics for notifications. These features ensure that researchers receive timely and relevant updates without being overwhelmed by information.
User Story

Video Platform

As a craft enthusiast, I want to explore new crafting techniques and project ideas while seamlessly shopping for the necessary supplies. The new video platform offers free, high-quality instructional videos that inspire me to start new projects. I can easily purchase materials featured in the videos directly from the platform, ensuring I have everything I need to get started without leaving the site.

Home Screen
The Home screen features a curated selection of the latest and most popular videos, offering immediate inspiration and easy access to new content. Users can quickly browse and start watching, with options to add videos to their playlists or favorites.
Episodic Video Screen
This screen displays a series of episodic videos, each focused on a specific craft project or technique. Users can follow along with the episodes, purchase related products directly, and save the series to their playlists for future reference.
My Playlists Screen
The My Playlists screen allows users to create and manage personalized playlists of their favorite videos. This feature helps users organize their learning journey and easily access their preferred content without searching.
Favorites Screen
The Favorites screen provides a collection of videos that users have marked as favorites. This section makes it easy to revisit and continue watching previously enjoyed content, keeping users engaged and inspired.
